How to use this checklist

Open the CHRONOS app at avionwatches.github.io/chronos-app alongside this page. Tap any test to expand it, follow the steps, then tap Pass, Fail or Skip.

Progress saves instantly every time you tap Pass, Fail or Skip. You will not lose progress if you close the page. The Save button force-saves at any time. Export downloads a text file report.
